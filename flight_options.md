# SFO → CTS Flight Options — January (Hokkaido Trip)

*Researched 2026-07-03. Prices are indicative ranges from aggregator data (Kayak, Expedia, Google Flights, airline sites) — confirm exact dates on Google Flights/ITA Matrix before booking.*

---

## The New United Direct Route
- **UA234/235, SFO ⇄ CTS, nonstop**, Boeing 787-9 (48 business / 21 premium economy / 188 economy)
- Seasonal: **Dec 11, 2026 – Mar 26, 2027** — covers the trip's January window
- Only **3x/week**: westbound Sun/Wed/Fri (SFO dep 10:30a, arr CTS 2:15p+1), eastbound Mon/Thu/Sat (CTS dep 4:15p, arr SFO 8:25a)
- Flight time ~10h45m out / 9h05m back — shortest true mainland-US–Japan transpacific route, first-ever nonstop CONUS–Sapporo service
- **Fare:** not yet officially published; early community estimates **~$2,000 round-trip economy** for Feb/Mar — likely the most expensive option, traded for zero connections and a daytime arrival (2:15pm lands in Sapporo in daylight, easier on Day 1 jet lag than a red-eye-plus-connection routing)
- **Constraint:** only 3 valid departure days/week each direction — the whole group's dates need to land on one of the 6 fixed weekly slots, not just "sometime in January"

## Option 1 — Cheapest: Budget carrier + separate domestic leg
**ZIPAIR (SFO or SJC) → NRT, then a Japanese LCC NRT → CTS**
- ZIPAIR SFO–NRT: ~**$420–$570 round-trip** in January (JAL's low-cost long-haul subsidiary); cheapest day historically Monday
- ZIPAIR also flies **SJC–NRT**, frequently **$50–130 cheaper from San Jose** than from SFO — worth checking as a nearby-airport swap for the group
- NRT → CTS domestic: Peach, Jetstar Japan, Air Do, or Spring Japan, roughly **$50–150 round-trip** booked separately
- **Estimated total: ~$470–$720/person round-trip**
- Trade-offs: two separate tickets (no protection if the domestic leg misconnects), no through checked bags, self-transfer at NRT, less certain seat-together for a group of 4, minimal/no free checked ski bags on the LCC domestic leg — a real issue for this trip given ski gear

## Option 2 — Best balance: One-stop through-fare on ANA or JAL via Tokyo
**SFO → HND/NRT → CTS, single ticket**
- Through-fares (one PNR, checked bags included, protected connection) commonly land at **~$900–$1,400 round-trip**, matching the current itinerary's flight budget
- Best domestic connection is via **Haneda (HND)** on ANA where possible — closer to central Tokyo, easier connection, frequent CTS domestic service
- **Korean Air / Asiana via Seoul (ICN)** is a comparable-priced one-stop alternative (SkyTeam, ~4h15m typical connection), similar **$900–$1,300** band — worth it mainly if the group has SkyTeam status/miles, otherwise no clear price edge over Tokyo routing
- Best routing for normal date flexibility (daily flights, unlike United's 3x/week) without the LCC self-transfer risk on ski bags

## Option 3 — New nonstop: United SFO–CTS direct
- ~$2,000 round-trip (unconfirmed/early estimate), 3x/week only — see baseline above
- Wins only on convenience (zero connections, no missed-bag risk on ski gear, predictable daytime arrival), not on price

---

## Day-of-week / booking-window flex (applies to Options 1 & 2 — United's schedule is fixed)
- January is already the **cheapest month** on this route per aggregator seasonality data
- **Thursday departures** run up to ~16% cheaper than Sunday on average
- Cheapest booking window is typically **~14 weeks out**; booking 5 weeks out still saves ~24% vs. last-minute
- Nearby-airport swap: **SJC over SFO** is worth a direct comparison for ZIPAIR pricing specifically; **OAK** showed no meaningfully cheaper Japan-bound service in this search and isn't worth the extra Bay Area routing complexity for a group of 4 with ski bags

## Bottom line ranking (cheapest → priciest)
1. **ZIPAIR + separate LCC domestic** — ~$470–$720/person, but real ski-bag/self-transfer risk for a group of 4
2. **ANA/JAL (or Korean Air via ICN) one-stop through-fare** — ~$900–$1,400/person, matches current itinerary budget, best balance of price/reliability — **recommended default**
3. **United SFO–CTS nonstop** — ~$2,000/person (est.), best convenience, worst price, fixed 3x/week schedule
